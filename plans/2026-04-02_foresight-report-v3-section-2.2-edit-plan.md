Focus: ensure the three analytical subparts (cross-impacts and uncertainty synthesis) work as an extension of the system map, not as separate essays, and that they maintain the child- and vulnerability-centred focus and sentence-level discipline of the style pack.

High-level objectives:

1. Strengthen linkage to the system map
- Make sure each cross-impact pair and each uncertainty is explicitly anchored to elements and loops described in 2.2 (economic–learning loop, trust–welfare loop, digital–learning/wellbeing loop, climate shocks, community networks).

2. Clarify paragraph missions and flow
- Ensure each subsection follows a clear sequence: context of the pair/uncertainty → mechanism (how the interaction works) → concrete effects on children and vulnerable groups → implication for future scenarios/strategy.

3. Emphasize vulnerable groups consistently

- Replace broad abstractions (เช่น “ความเสี่ยงซ้อนความเสี่ยง”, “ผลกระทบระยะยาว”) with more specific formulations tied to learning continuity, income stability, access to protection, or mental health.
