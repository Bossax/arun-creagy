## Deliverable pack (what must exist immediately after the workshop)

### A) NFCS Steering Decision Package (≤2 pages)
Format: **recommendations for steering endorsement**.

Minimum content:
1) Priority service bundles (Human Settlement & Security): select 1–2 bundles to carry into NFCS validation.
2) Minimum baseline + caveat rules: endorsed baseline vs candidate vs interpreted products (details in annex).
3) Implementing role map: owner/steward/custodian/reviewer/approver, explicitly **“under NFCS governance”**.
4) Operating rhythm: review moments and update cadence.
5) NCAIF positioning statement: “enables cross-agency service delivery and auditability; does not replace line agencies.”

### B) NCAIF website blueprint annex (TOR-literal)
Minimum components:
1) Sitemap (organize by **service bundles**, not datasets)
2) Page blueprint template:
   - Purpose (decision supported)
   - Target user tier
   - Inputs: baseline datasets + caveats
   - Outputs: products/services
   - Provenance + update rhythm
   - Owner/steward contacts
3) Crosswalk table: pages ↔ use-case clusters ↔ baseline datasets ↔ responsible agencies
