## 2. 2.1.1 สัญญาณแนวโน้มหลักและปัจจัยขับเคลื่อนสำคัญ (Signals & Drivers)

### Gap Analysis
- **Structural Imbalance:** The comment section (% sign) contains a list of important signals (Nationalism, Geopolitics, Land law, Zakat/Waqf, etc.) that are *not* yet integrated into the prose.
- **Weak Weighting:** Digital and Climate are highly developed in the current prose, but the Benchmark warns they are "elevated beyond concept-note base." Conversely, "Malay Muslim identity, language, and culturally meaningful learning" are under-represented in the integrated prose despite being team anchors.
- **Classification Clarity:** Does not clearly separate "Structural," "Dynamic," and "Emergent" signals as required by the Benchmark.
- **Abstract Logic:** Some points (e.g., Signal 5: Trust) are described as a "institutional condition" but lack concrete examples of *how* this manifests in a child's day (Style Pack 1.3).
- **Missing Connection:** The "Halal Economy" signal (Signal 106) in the scratchpad is a critical bridge to livelihoods but is not explained as a *mechanism* (Benchmark Strategic Note).

### Proposed Changes
- **Integrate the Scratchpad:** Move signals like "Nationalism," "Land Law," and "Halal Economy" into the main prose.
- **Rebalance Weighting:** 
	- Subordinate Digital and Climate to supporting roles.
	- Elevate Education Pathways, Identity/Culture, and Trust/Safety to lead drivers. %% please do it!  %%
