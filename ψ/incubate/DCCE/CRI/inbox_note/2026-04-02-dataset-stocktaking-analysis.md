
# loss23Y_DDPM

The data has been converted into a structured CSV format with the following standardized column headers:
- `province_th`: Province name (Thai)
- `flood_loss`: Losses/Relief for Floods (อุทกภัย)
- `drought_loss`: Losses/Relief for Drought (ภัยแล้ง)
- `dry_spell_loss`: Losses/Relief for Dry Spells (ฝนทิ้งช่วง)
- `cold_wave_loss`: Losses/Relief for Cold Waves (ภัยหนาว)
- `storm_loss`: Losses/Relief for Windstorms (วาตภัย)
- `total_loss`: Combined disaster relief expenditure over 23 years.
    
### Initial Data Observations:
From a public policy and resilience perspective, the top 5 provinces by total disaster relief expenditure are:
1. **Pathum Thani**: 6.89 Billion THB
2. **Nonthaburi**: 4.79 Billion THB
3. **Phra Nakhon Si Ayutthaya**: 3.73 Billion THB
4. **Tak**: 3.13 Billion THB
5. **Bangkok**: 3.11 Billion THB
