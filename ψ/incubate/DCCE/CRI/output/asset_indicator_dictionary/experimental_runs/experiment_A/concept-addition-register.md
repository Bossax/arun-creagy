# Experiment A Concept-Addition Register

## Result

No provisional new governance concept was admitted in Experiment A.

The Round 2 review tested whether seven unresolved activation problems required expansion beyond the v3 kernel. The evidence package did not justify a surviving seventh concept. All seven gaps were carried by interpretation notes or by controlled combinations of existing v3 concepts. A live NotebookLM extraction corroborated that most asset families map cleanly to the six readiness categories and marked only food-system stock and human/social capability stock as `unclear`, not as confirmed seventh-concept cases.
