## 5) News and Public Services (engagement cluster)

The website uses News categories for public relations, project updates, CCE updates, procurement, and recruitment. Public Services includes training, e‑learning, and public access systems.

## 6) Observed structural characteristics (as‑is)

- The portal is **administration‑first** in navigation, with content clustered by organizational or program silos.
- Technical data systems are **distributed across subdomains**, creating a fragmented discovery experience.
- Adaptation content exists inside the Central Information Center but is **document‑centric** rather than workflow‑centric.
- Risk data is present (Risk MAP) but does not yet connect to an adaptation‑cycle narrative or a unified policy‑maker front door.
