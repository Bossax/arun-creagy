# NCAIF Information Architecture (Workshop Edition v3)
**Goal**: A detailed sitemap reflecting Climate Service principles, grounded in official Thai datasets and frameworks.

---

## 1. Home: National Climate Resilience Portal
*Identity: The "High-Level Entry Point" for executives and the public.*

### 1.1 Thailand's Climate Risk Overview
*   **Overview**: Summary of Thailand's climate profile.
*   **Data Grounding**: [Thailand Climate Knowledge Assessment Report]
    *   **Natural Hazard History**: Historical extremes (1960–2014) from TMD and GISTDA.
    *   **Physical Risk Summary**: Based on IPCC AR6 framework (Hazard x Exposure x Vulnerability).
    *   **Vision**: DCCE's role in the "Quiet Nudge" for institutional data reuse.

### 1.2 Key Climate Risks & Adaptation Priorities
*   **High Risk Impacts**: Sectoral "Hotspots" (e.g., Central Plain floods, Northeast droughts).
*   **NAP Process**: 6-sector focus (Water, Agri, Tourism, Health, Natural Resources, Human Settlement).
*   **Featured Insight Cards**: e.g., "The Economic Cost of 1.5°C in Thai Agri Sector."

## 2. Center for Policy Makers
*Identity: Support for high-stakes decisions and reporting (MVP-1 & MVP-2).*

### 2.1 Summary of Risks by Area and Sector
*   **National Summary**: World Bank/DCCE joint insights.
*   **Provincial Profiles**: [Spatial Risk Database engine]
    *   **Decision Utility**: Interactive "Hotspot" mapping for provincial governors.
    *   **Note**: Need to validate if "Risk Determinants" (S/AC) or "Impacts" (L&D) should be the primary message.
*   **Sector Summaries**: [BTR/A-BTR] Synthetic summaries of the 6 NAP sectors.

### 2.2 National Situation 
*Identity: The "National Monitoring Loop" for tracking climate impact, threat, and response.*

*   **A) Climate Impact & Disaster Statistics (Past/Current Reality)**:
    *   **Focus**: [MVP-2] Re-indexing DDPM relief statistics and loss data.
    *   **Utility**: Monitoring **Climate-related Loss and Damage** — the historical and immediate economic and social costs associated with reactive relief and climate events.
*   **B) Climate Risk & Vulnerability Outlook (Future/Residual Risk)**:
    *   **Focus**: [Spatial Risk Database / CRI]
    *   **Utility**: Assessing the "Threat Outlook" — showing the shifting risk landscape and **Residual Risk** (the risk remaining after current adaptation measures are considered).
*   **C) National Adaptation Progress (Implementation/Response)**:
    *   **Focus**: [TDRI/NAP M&E Framework]
    *   **Utility**: Tracking "Investment Performance" — monitoring how effectively policy implementation is reducing the vulnerability identified in Part B.

### 2.3 Data Services for Strategic Planning
*   **Hazard & Vulnerability Overlay Tool**: 
	* ADPC Climate Hazard Map integration
	* DCCE spatial risk map
*   **Data Methodology & Uncertainty (MVP-4)**:
    *   **Content**: How to interpret **Decision Readiness Labels** (e.g., "Ready for Infrastructure Design").
    *   **Grounding**: IPCC Linguistic scales for confidence.

## 3. Adaptation Information by Cycle
*Identity: The "Knowledge Hub" for researchers and practitioners.*

### 3.1 Climate Science (Historical & Projections)
*   **Historical Observations**:
    *   **Climate**: TMD/HII/AWS historical trends.
    *   **Systems Baseline**: GISTDA Remote-Sensing products:
        *   Forest Cover (Yearly), Land Cover/Land Use (LULC).
        *   Freshwater bodies (Surface water mapping).
        *   Coral Reef/Coastal Monitoring (Satellite-based).
*   **Climate Drivers & Trends**:
    *   **Grounding**: RU-CORE downscaled CMIP6 (25x25 km, scaling to 5x5 km).
    *   **Scenarios**: SSP2-4.5 (Moderate) and SSP5-8.5 (High-Emission).
*   **Future Climate Scenarios**:
    *   **Content**: Explainer on Scenario Planning (Win-Win Strategies) vs. "Predict-then-Act."

### 3.2 Risks and Impacts
*   **Exposure & Vulnerability**:
    *   **Grounding**: [CRI Phase 1 Report]
    *   **Concepts**: Damage functions, social vulnerability, and adaptive capacity (AC) factors (e.g., hospital density, early warning coverage).
*   **Risk Assessments (Sectoral & Area-Based)**:
    *   **Content**: Detailed drill-downs for Agri, Tourism, Water.
*   **Impact Chain Analysis**: Case studies of cascading impacts (e.g., Flood -> Transport -> Agri Supply Chain).

### 3.3 Loss and Damage
*   **L&D Framework**: UNFCCC Warsaw Mechanism overview.
*   **National L&D Statistics (MVP-2)**: Direct link to the "Disaster-Climate Pipeline."

### 3.4 Adaptation Planning & Options
*   **Adaptation Options Library**:
    *   **Nature-based Solutions (NbS)**: Mangrove restoration, living shorelines.
    *   **Infrastructure**: Flood walls, heat-resilient buildings.
    *   **Policy/Community**: Early warning systems, insurance mechanisms.
*   **Planning Case Examples**:
    *   **Grounding**: [TGO Provincial Climate Change Master Plan learnings].
    *   **Content**: "How to translate a Risk Map into a Budget Request."

### 3.5 Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)
*   **Adaptation M&E Platform**: Integrated view of project tracking (M&E Platform website).
*   **Adaptation Stories**: Qualitative "Success Narratives" from the field.
