# TOR 5.2.3 – NCAIF Use-Case to Component Mapping

This matrix maps representative use-case clusters from [`ψ/incubate/DCCE/CRDB/output/NCAIF_Use_Cases.md`](ψ/incubate/DCCE/CRDB/output/NCAIF_Use_Cases.md) to NCAIF structural components and MVPs.

| Use-case cluster (examples) | Primary users (from use-case note) | NCAIF components (sections / pages) | Related MVP(s) | Notes on mapping (evidence-based) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Policy and budget briefing (e.g. UC-03 provincial/LAO budget justification; policy-maker overviews) | Policy makers; provincial planners; budget officers | Policy Maker Centre (national and provincial summaries); Adaptation Information by Cycle → planning segment; Risk and Area Profiles; decision-maker briefs layer | MVP-1 – Briefing Pack Generator; MVP-3 – Recommended Dataset Registry | Use cases emphasise exportable, printable packs and clear baselines. NCAIF provides briefing-pack pages that draw on recommended baselines and risk/area profiles while exposing limitations and provenance. |
| Post-event impact and loss assessment (UC-01/UC-09 style patterns) | DDPM, NESDC, DCCE analysts | Adaptation Information by Cycle → Loss and Damage; event pages; economic loss explainer; Risk and Area Profiles (for baseline context) | MVP-2 – Post-event reporting pack; MVP-3 – Recommended Dataset Registry | Use cases require linking events to baselines and estimating “true” economic loss. NCAIF components provide event pages, baseline references, and post-event packs, underpinned by the event/impact schema and governance rules. |
| Local planning and service targeting (UC-03, UC-04, UC-07b clusters) | LAO/municipality planners; social protection planners; city operators | Risk and Area Profiles; area profile templates; Adaptation Information by Cycle → planning and implementation; Knowledge/Tools/Data Services for boundary and dataset guidance | MVP-1 – Briefing packs for local planning; MVP-3 – Recommended baseline registry; MVP-4 – Uncertainty and boundary guidance | Use cases describe the need for area-based evidence packs, boundary/crosswalk rules, and exportable justifications. NCAIF components provide profiles, packs, and governance-guidance pages that clarify how to use risk indices and baselines safely. |
| Baseline verification and clearinghouse (UC-10 and related governance items) | DCCE central data/governance roles; NXPO; cross-agency integrators | Knowledge/Tools/Data Services → Recommended Baseline Registry pages; metadata and limitation guidance; governance explainer pages | MVP-3 – Recommended Baseline Registry; MVP-4 – Uncertainty guidance and publishing standard | The use-case note defines a clearinghouse model where DCCE endorses baselines and publishes usage guidance. NCAIF exposes this via registry and guidance pages, while the CDM and governance notes manage the underlying data model and decision rights. |
| Financial-sector and advanced analytic use (UC-11; high-resolution analytic users) | Bank risk analysts; advanced technical users | Knowledge/Tools/Data Services → advanced-data and guidance pages; not front-page navigation; Adaptation Information by Cycle → technical annexes and methods | MVP-3 – access to foundational layers; MVP-4 – uncertainty guidance | The use cases indicate that NCAIF should not promise bank-grade modelling in Phase 1. Instead, NCAIF maps these users to foundational datasets, clear uncertainty guidance, and catalog entries that indicate where more advanced models live or may be integrated later. |
