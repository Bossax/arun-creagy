# TOR 5.2.2 – DCCE Current Structure Snapshot Table

| Unit/Section / System                                                   | Role (as-is)                                                                                                     | Systems/Products                                                                                                               | Relevance to NCAIF                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| DCCE central climate/adaptation units (policy and technical teams)      | Produce and sponsor climate-risk and adaptation information products; coordinate internal climate policy content | National reports, thematic adaptation studies, guidance documents published as PDFs on the DCCE website                        | Provide **source content** and thematic ownership for NCAIF policy and knowledge layers; their existing reports will be reorganized into decision-oriented summaries and briefings rather than re-created from scratch.                                                   |
| DCCE web and communications team                                        | Maintain the main DCCE website structure and public-facing pages                                                 | Main DCCE website, section pages, news items, and links to sub-portals                                                         | Act as the **host environment** for NCAIF pages; current navigation and content patterns explain why NCAIF must introduce clearer entry points and adaptation-cycle narratives while remaining within the DCCE web estate.                                                |

| CCIC and related climate information systems                            | Provide access to climate indicators, emissions information, and related data tools                              | CCIC portal and associated dashboards/tools linked from the DCCE website                                                       | Contribute **climate and emissions data services** that will sit underneath NCAIF’s climate and risk foundations; current fragmentation across portals motivates a consolidated discovery and cataloguing function within NCAIF.                                          |
| Program-specific portals (e.g. SCP, thematic initiatives)               | Communicate and manage specific climate or environment programs                                                  | SCP portals and other project-specific microsites                                                                              | Hold **case studies, measures, and program information** that are relevant to NCAIF’s adaptation-measure and implementation views; today these assets are scattered, so NCAIF must index and link to them rather than duplicate them.                                     |
| External data-owning agencies (DDPM, DGA, NSO, sectoral ministries)     | Own and maintain core hazard, exposure, socio-economic, and administrative datasets                              | National hazard datasets, socio-economic statistics, registries, and platforms accessed via data.go.th, GDX, or other channels | Provide the **underlying datasets** that DCCE and NCAIF rely on; DCCE acts as a user and convenor rather than sole owner. NCAIF’s data-management and governance structure must respect this distributed ownership while defining how recommended baselines are endorsed. |
| Central digital/IT infrastructure (data.go.th, GDX, government hosting) | Provide rails and hosting for open data, G2G exchange, and internal systems                                      | Open data portal, government data exchange, and internal hosting environments                                                  | Define the **publishing rails** (open, G2G, internal) within which DCCE can share climate-risk data. NCAIF’s management structure will align with these rails rather than creating a separate technical stack.                                                            |

This table is intended to support brief paragraphs in Section 1.2 / 1.6 that describe the **current institutional and website structure** around climate-risk information. All cells are populated with concrete, as-is roles so that the interim report does not need to reconstruct this mapping during drafting.
