MVPs are now not so different from workflow patterns.

Workflow patterns should stress the general procedural steps data users take when using data to do something (use cases), while MVPs are the self‑contained entities that help users achieve their goals when following these workflows.

MVP 2 should also be framed as groundwork for future development. MVP 2 is an effort to ingest disaster‑related data from DDPM into DCCE’s boundary, even though ingestion is not automatic. DCCE can take this opportunity to develop data pipelines to tailor disaster information products to their needs and establish data governance on disaster and Loss & Damage domain (part of the CDM).
