## FGD2 signal: “Sitemap = platform” framing dominates decision-maker expectations

In today’s FGD2 meeting, DCCE officials were heavily focused on the **sitemap**.

- Director Nid (lead of the Climate Change Adaptation Division) emphasized that DCCE wants:
  - **baseline data inventory**, and
  - a **“website”** for climate change adaptation information.
- In the mental model of decision makers, **“website” ≈ information platform**.
- They were initially **less aware of the importance of backend design** (CDM, logic, product design).

Response from participants:

- Overall **positive**.
- Their questions were answered.
- They understood:
  - why we need CDM and how it supports frontend / sitemap design,
  - the advantage of user-journey-oriented design,
  - the need for data governance.
