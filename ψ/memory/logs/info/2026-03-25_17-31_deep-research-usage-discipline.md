Deep Research usage discipline for CRDB and related work:

- Deep Research is resource-intensive and should be used **only when it enables a major leap forward** in understanding, strategy, or evidence synthesis.
- For topics where sufficient material already exists in local notes, interviews, FGDs, or prior synthesis artifacts, **do not invoke Deep Research**. Instead, work from existing project knowledge.
- Treat Deep Research as an escalation tool for genuinely novel, unclear, or cross-jurisdictional questions, not as the default first step.
