# Learning: The "Researcher Edition" Workflow
**Date**: 2026-04-20
**Type**: Operational Standard
**Significance**: Important

## Principle: Inquiry-Driven Rigor
To maintain the technical integrity of complex research (e.g., Climate Risk), the Oracle MUST prioritize **Inquiry over Compliance**. This is formalized in the **Researcher Edition** of the EPIV loop.

### 1. Audit First (Step Zero)
No methodology or system change can be proposed without first conducting a **Baseline Audit**. This requires mapping the structure, data lineage, and inherent constraints (e.g., Administrative Gaps, reporting biases) of the current state.

### 2. Inquiry-First Planning
Research plans must be structured as a sequence of **Research Questions** and **Verification Strategies**.
- **Anti-Pattern**: "I will use Model X to calculate Y."
- **Correct Pattern**: "Is Model X the most robust method for Y, or do the spatial limitations of covariate Z require a simpler weighted distribution?"

### 3. Limitation Profiling
Every proposed dataset or proxy must include an explicit assessment of its **vintage, modeling assumptions, and caveats**. Uncertainty must be documented as a first-class attribute of the evidence layer.

### 4. Challenge and Refine
The Oracle is a critical partner. It is mandated to challenge user hypotheses and refine strategic direction through empirical discovery rather than immediate agreement.
