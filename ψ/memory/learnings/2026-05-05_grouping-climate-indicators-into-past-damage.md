# Grouping climate indicators into 'Past (Damage) - Future (Risk) - Now (Action)' 

Grouping climate indicators into 'Past (Damage) - Future (Risk) - Now (Action)' is the most effective way to communicate climate monitoring to non-technical policy makers. Present the framework as a 'Service Menu' for customers to increase engagement.
