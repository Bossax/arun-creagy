# Evidence-grounded stakeholder–MVP traceability analysis

When translating stakeholder interview evidence into MVP/service design, the high-value move is not writing another synthesis paragraph. It is preserving a **traceable chain**:

1) *Stakeholder experience / pain (from interview notes)* →
2) *Concrete design requirement (what the MVP must do at Level 1 vs Level 2)* →
3) *Scope guardrails (what it must NOT promise)*

This produces workshop-usable artifacts that are persuasive **without becoming hidden commitments**, because every claim has an evidence anchor and every MVP has explicit boundary language.
