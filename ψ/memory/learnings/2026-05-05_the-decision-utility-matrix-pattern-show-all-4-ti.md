# The Decision Utility Matrix pattern: Show all 4 tiers of administrative decision

The Decision Utility Matrix pattern: Show all 4 tiers of administrative decision-making (Scoping, Budgeting, Zoning, Design) simultaneously to enforce 'Decision Guardrails'. This prevents the misuse of high-uncertainty data for high-precision tasks while still highlighting its strategic value.
